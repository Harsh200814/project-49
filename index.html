Intersting facts about camel

1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
2. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can’t eat.
3. Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.

Intersting facts about chameleon

1. Chameleons have eyes that can swivel. They can look at two different things at once.
2. The panther chameleon changes its color to yellow or red when it’s angry.When a chameleon is cold, it will become darker in color. Dark colors attract heat better than light ones.
3. Sick chameleons look pale in color.

Intersting facts about pigeon

1. Pigeons can navigate via the earth’s magnetic fields
2. Pigeons always lay at least 2 eggs with each one being a male and a female
3. Pigeons have been known to live over 30 years

Intersting facts about Teddy Bear

1. The first toy bear is believed to be made by German toy maker Margarete Steiff in 1902 that was a jointed,stuffed one.
2. American multi-billionaire investor Paul Greenwood owned the largest collection of teddy bears.
3. Japanese company Fujitsu has manufactured a teddy bear robot for adults that can make simple gestures and even small talk with humans.

Intersting facts about panda

1. They have great camouflage for their environment.
2. Their eyes are different to normal bears.
3. They spend a lot of their day eating.

Intersting facts about flying cicada

1. They are about the size of a grain of rice when they drop from a tree branch to the ground and start digging.
2. Most have red-orange eyes. But occasionally cicadas have blue, white or grayish eyes. Keep on the lookout.
3. They improve lawns by digging tunnels that allow air into the soil. When they die, their rotting bodies put nitrogen into the soil.
